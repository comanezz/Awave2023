Ocean Co. Partnership

We're so excited and proud to partner with Ocean Co, an incredible organization that is on the front lines of protecting our oceans from plastic pollution.

The ocean is a beautiful, vast place. It's home to an abundance of life and experiences, and it connects us all.

But the ocean is also in trouble. Over 8 million tons of plastic enters our oceans every year—and that number is growing at an alarming rate. It's not just a problem for the future, it's a problem now.

We truly care about the future of our oceans and planet. By owning an AWAVE garment, you're contributing to this fight and helping create a wave of change.
